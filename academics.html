Pre-School Department

The first department to ever be established in Colegio Sto. Domingo, The Pre-school department, in accordance with the MATATAG curriculum, aims to create students who are ready to take on what the world has to offer, while also maintaining their care and regard for their peers, the environment, and the community.



Subjects

-Mathematics
-Reading and Literacy
-Makabansa
-Language
-Physical and Natural Environment
-Good Manners and Right Conduct (GMRC)



Extracurriculars

-Girl Scouts and Boy Scouts (GSP & BSP)





Primary Department

Established in 1996, The primary department follows the MATATAG curriculum as set by DEPED, focusing primarily on developing students who are not only intelligent, but responsible and upstanding individuals in society.



Subjects

-Mathematics
-Reading and Literacy
-Makabansa
-Language
-Physical and Natural Environment
-Good Manners and Right Conduct (GMRC)

Extracurriculars

-Girl Scouts and Boy Scouts (GSP & BSP)



Middle School Department

Alongside the Primary department, The Middle school department, established in 1996, follows the MATATAG curriculum as per DEPED’s ensuring students are equipped with the knowledge and skills to adapt and succeed in secondary education, while also guaranteeing they have real world abilities usable not just in their future careers and academics, but also in their daily lives.

Subjects

-Edukasyong Pantahan at Pangkabuhayan (EPP)
-Mathematics
-English
-AP
-Filipino
-Good Manners and Right Conduct
-Music and Art
-Physical Education (P.E) and Health
-Science

Extracurriculars

-Girl Scouts & Boy Scouts (GSP & BSP)
-Varsity & Interschool competitions
-Clubs (vary per school year)
-Intramurals
-Volunteer & Charity work
-Outside of school activities (Camps, Fieldtrips, etc)



Junior High School Department

Established in 1984, The High school department currently abides by the curriculums set by DEPED, namely the K12 Curriculum (Grade 9-12) and the MATATAG curriculum (Grades 7-8), ensuring students get the necessary information and skills to excel and succeed in a rapidly changing world, as envisioned by our school and its founder.

Subjects

-Araling Panlipunan -Science -English -Filipino -MAPEH -Mathematics -TLE -Food and Consumer Sciences (FCS) -Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) -Values Education

Extracurriculars

-Varsity & Interschool competitions
-Clubs (vary per school year)
-Intramurals
-Volunteer & Charity work
-Outside of school activities (Field trips, Theater Viewing, etc)
-Career Guidance week

Senior High School Strands

-Accounting and Business Management (ABM) -Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)
